## 📌 What is this project?

This repository contains the semester-long systems analysis and design project for the course **Systems Analysis & Design (2026-I)**. Our assigned system is a **Smart Campus Parking Management System (SCPMS)** — a proposed IoT-enabled solution to solve the chronic motorcycle parking problem at the university's main campus.

### The Problem
The motorcycle parking area in **Basement 3 (Sótano 3)** has no physical cell demarcation. This means a single motorcycle can occupy the space of three, making real-time availability impossible to know without physically going down there — and even then, security staff are not always present to guide users.

### Our Proposed Solution
A digital reservation and monitoring platform integrated into the **existing university mobile app** that allows students to:
- View a **numbered real-time map** of Basement 3
- **Reserve a specific space** for their academic time slot
- Receive or release reservations based on their schedule
- Alert security staff automatically when a space is violated
